Heart Of Stone

On Heart of Stone, I was part of the compositing team, responsible for cleanup, greenscreen removal, and integration of CG and practical elements into live-action shots. My work involved removing rigs and markers, keying actors from greenscreen backgrounds, and ensuring that all added elements matched the lighting and motion of the original footage for a seamless final composite.
